Figtory was founded in 2011; its primary focus was using open source and cloud based infrastructures to enrich the digital experience. As the market portfolio grew, the focus became broader and included 3D modelling for architecture and engineering purposes. We have also evolved our competencies around info-graphics and animation; to involve data visualisation frameworks, web 3.0 as our primary focus in context with the web infrastructure evolution. We aim to take advantage of new developments around web 3.0 built of more solid semantics and better access channels such as augmented reality and 3D printing.
